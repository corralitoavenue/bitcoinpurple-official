# BitcoinPurple Official

Official BitcoinPurple repositories: node, stratum, wallet, and ecosystem tools.

BitcoinPurple is a decentralized digital currency that enables fast, low-cost transactions without the need for intermediaries or central authorities.  
Derived from the Bitcoin protocol, BitcoinPurple focuses on improving efficiency and transaction speed while preserving the core principles of decentralization and security.

BitcoinPurple operates on a peer-to-peer network using blockchain technology to record and verify transactions.  
Transactions are transparent, irreversible, and resistant to censorship, giving users full control over their funds.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🔹 Scarcity
The maximum supply of BitcoinPurple is **1,000,000 BTCP**, ensuring long-term scarcity.

---

### 🔹 Decentralization
BitcoinPurple runs on a fully decentralized network of nodes, eliminating single points of failure and preventing censorship or centralized control.

---

### 🔹 Proof of Work (SHA-256)
BitcoinPurple uses the **SHA-256 Proof of Work algorithm**, the same as Bitcoin.  
This allows mining with existing ASIC hardware and leverages a well-tested and secure consensus mechanism.

---

### 🔹 Fair Launch (No Premine)
BitcoinPurple launched with **no premine**, ensuring fair and transparent distribution from the genesis block.

- Initial block reward: **1 BTCP**  
- Halving: every **500,000 blocks**

---

### 🔹 Fast Transactions
BitcoinPurple features a **1-minute block time**, significantly faster than Bitcoin’s 10 minutes, enabling quicker confirmations.

---

### 🔹 Coinbase Maturity
Mining rewards require **100 confirmations** before they can be spent, improving network security and stability.

---

### 🔹 Difficulty Adjustment
BitcoinPurple adjusts mining difficulty **every hour**, ensuring network stability even during rapid hashrate fluctuations.

---

### 🔹 Open Source
BitcoinPurple is fully open source and designed to be transparent and community-driven.
